# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Overview

Built an end-to-end data analytics project using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to analyze customer shopping behavior across 3,900 transactions. The objective was to uncover spending patterns, customer segments, product preferences, and subscription trends to support data-driven business decisions.

**Workflow:** Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering (Python) → Business Analysis (MySQL) → Dashboard Development (Power BI) → Business Recommendations

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographics, purchase information, and shopping behavior data.

| Feature Group     | Columns                                                                                    |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Demographics      | Age, Gender, Location, Subscription Status                                                 |
| Purchase Details  | Item Purchased, Category, Purchase Amount                                                  |
| Shopping Behavior | Review Rating, Discount Applied, Previous Purchases, Frequency of Purchases, Shipping Type |

**Dataset Size:** 3,900 Rows × 18 Columns

## 🔍 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering

* Handled missing review ratings using category-wise median imputation.
* Standardized column names using snake_case naming conventions.
* Created customer age groups for segmentation analysis.
* Converted purchase frequency categories into numerical values.
* Removed redundant columns after data consistency checks.

### 2. SQL Business Analysis

Performed business-focused analysis to answer key questions such as:

* Revenue by gender
* Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
* Subscriber vs Non-Subscriber behavior
* Revenue by age group
* Product performance analysis
* Discount dependency analysis
* Shipping type comparison
* Top-rated products

### 3. Power BI Dashboard

Developed an interactive dashboard featuring:

* Customer overview KPIs
* Revenue and sales by category
* Revenue and sales by age group
* Subscription analysis
* Interactive filters for gender, category, subscription status, and shipping type

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Male customers generated approximately **68% of total revenue**.
* Loyal customers represented the largest customer segment.
* Subscribers and non-subscribers showed nearly identical average spending per order.
* Clothing generated the highest revenue and sales volume.
* Young Adult and Middle-aged customers contributed the highest revenue.
* Express shipping customers spent slightly more on average.
* Hats, Sneakers, and Coats showed the highest dependence on discounts.
* Gloves, Sandals, and Boots received the highest average customer ratings.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase subscription adoption through exclusive member benefits.
* Strengthen customer loyalty programs to retain repeat buyers.
* Reassess discount strategies for highly discount-dependent products.
* Promote top-rated products through targeted marketing campaigns.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments and categories.
